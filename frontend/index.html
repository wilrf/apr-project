NFL Upset Prediction

A stripped-down reading edition of the model comparison dashboard. The emphasis here is on the evidence, not the chrome.

Research Question How do the agreement and disagreement patterns of three structurally distinct machine learning architectures reveal the mechanisms and predictability boundaries of NFL upsets?

LR

Logistic Regression reads a single statistical snapshot of each matchup. It is the plainest model in the set and the easiest one to interpret directly.

XGB

XGBoost reads the same matchup plus short rolling game histories. It is built to find combinations and interaction effects that a linear model will miss.

LSTM

The LSTM reads each team as a recent sequence. It is the temporal model and is meant to pick up shifts in form, momentum, and trajectory.
